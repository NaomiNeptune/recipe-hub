Red Velvet Cookies



Ingredients:
1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
½ cup butter
⅔ cup granulated sugar
¼ cup brown sugar
½ teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons cocoa powder
1 ½ teaspoon red food coloring (optional)
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
4 tablespoons greek yogurt
Steps:
1. Beat the butter, sugar, cocoa powder, salt, food coloring, vanilla extract, and heavy cream together until fluffy and well-combined.
2. Add the flour and beat until just combined
3. Allow the dough to rest for an hour in the refrigerator so the flour may hydrate. If you taste it immediately after mixing it won't taste right. It needs to rest.
