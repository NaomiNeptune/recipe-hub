Ingredients:
-3 cups all-purpose flour
-7 teaspoons baking powder
-1/2 teaspoon salt
-2 tablespoons white sugar

-2 eggs
-6 tablespoons butter, melted
Steps:
1. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle.
