Ingredients
-¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
-¾ cup (12 tablespoons) melted butter
-1 ½ cups white sugar
-2 eggs
-¼ teaspoon salt
-1 cup all-purpose flour
-2 teaspoons vanilla extract
-⅓ cup powdered sugar for decoration
Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Line one 9x13 inch pan with parchment paper.
2. Melt the butter and add the sugar, vanilla, and eggs. In a separate bowl mix the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Now mix the two together.
3. Spread mixture into the prepared pan. Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
